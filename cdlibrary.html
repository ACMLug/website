Lug is proud to announce that a small collection of Linux CD's have been made available in the ACM Office (1104 Siebel Center). Anyone may borrow them simply by walking into the office and asking to see the library. Due to a few people not returning the CD-r's we have started a checkout system. You must include your name, email address, and the distribution you are checking out on a form prior to borrowing the CDs. All we ask is that you return them within 48 hours.

If you come into the ACM office and the distro listed here that you want isn't available, try back again in a couple of days. If it still isn't available let us know and we'll make more to meet demand (within reason). If you want a distro which isn't included here please let us know and we'll consider including it.

Here is a brief list of available Linux/BSD distributions for borrowing in 1104 Siebel Center:

  • Knoppix v3.6 English
  • Suse 9.1 Professional
  • Fedora Core 2 (Discs 1,2,3,4)
  • Fedora Core 2 AMD64 (DVD)
  • Mandrake Linux 10.1 (Disc 1-3)
  • Debian Linux 3.0r2 i386 Disc 1 (2 copies)
  • Debian Linux 3.0r2 i386 Discs 2-7
  • Debian Linux 3.0r2 PPC Disc 1
  • Gentoo Linux 2004.2
  • Yellow Dog 3.0.1 (for PPC, discs 1,2)
