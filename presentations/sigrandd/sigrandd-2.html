2. Advantages and Disadvantages of Using Named Pipes (FIFOs)

2.1 Advantages

  • Every application gets transparent access to a randomized .signature
  • We are guaranteed that the .signature will change every time it is accessed, if so desired. (This is not true for, say, crontab-based .signature randomizers)
  • We are allowed infinite control over the contents of .signature.

2.2 Disadvantages

  • If the signature randomizing daemon is not running, reading .signature will block forever.
  • The daemon must be running essentially 24/7 (which is probably not acceptable on machines you do not own)
