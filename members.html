Interested in becoming a member?

If you're interested in becoming a member, all you need to do is show up at a weekly meeting! LUG meets Tuesday nights at 8:00pm. Meetings are very informal, open to everyone, and usually cover current Linux happenings, as well as a few minutes set aside for Q & A.
